close

The 'close' method in Go is used to close resources such as files, network connections, or channels. It is critical for managing resources efficiently, ensuring that they are released when they are no longer needed. When a resource is closed, it may prevent further operations on it and allow for cleanup of underlying resources. Proper use of the 'close' method helps to avoid resource leaks and improves application stability.
