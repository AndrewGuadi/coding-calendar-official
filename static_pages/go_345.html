close

The 'close' method in Go is primarily used to close a resource such as a file, a network connection, or a database session. Closing a resource releases any system resources associated with it and ensures that any buffered data is flushed properly. It's crucial to call 'close' on resources when they are no longer needed to prevent resource leaks, which can lead to exhaustion of system resources and undefined behavior in your application.
